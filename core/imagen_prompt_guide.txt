# Imagen 4 Prompt Guide (Google Official)

## Core Structure
Combine three elements: Subject (main object/person) + Context/Background (setting) + Style (artistic approach).

## Photography Techniques
- Camera Proximity: close-up, zoomed out
- Camera Position: aerial photo, from below
- Lighting: natural lighting, dramatic lighting, warm/cold tones
- Camera Settings: motion blur, soft focus, bokeh, portrait mode
- Lens Types: 35mm, 50mm, fisheye, wide angle, macro
- Film Types: black and white, polaroid

## Photorealistic Guidelines
- Portraits: 24-35mm lens, depth of field, film noir, duotone
- Objects/Still Life: macro lens 60-105mm, high detail, precise focusing, controlled lighting
- Motion: telephoto zoom 100-400mm, fast shutter speed, movement tracking
- Landscapes: wide-angle 10-24mm, long exposure, sharp focus, smooth water/clouds

## Quality Modifiers
- General: high-quality, beautiful, stylized
- Photography: 4K, HDR, studio photo, professional photography
- Art: by a professional, detailed

## Art Styles
- Prefix: "A painting of..." or "A sketch of..."
- Techniques: charcoal, pastel, digital art, art deco, technical pencil
- Materials: "made of [material]", "in the shape of [object]"
- Movements: impressionism, renaissance, pop art

## Key Rules
- Max prompt length: 480 tokens
- Keep prompts descriptive with adjectives and adverbs
- Use "portrait" for face-focused shots
- Do NOT include text/words/letters in image prompts
- Vertical composition (9:16) for YouTube Shorts
- Start simple, iterate toward complexity
- Each prompt should have ONE clear main subject
